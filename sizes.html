Grazing Table Size Options

Grazing tables are priced based on the size and approximate number of people you intend to feed.

Small Grazing Table

Perfect for intimate celebrations of 6 to 10 people.
Requires about 3 to 4 feet of tabletop space.
Recommended grazing table package: The Garnet Gathering.

Medium Grazing Table

Feeds 10 to 15 people.
Requires about 4 to 6 feet of tabletop space.
Recommended grazing table package: The Marble Masterpiece.

Large Grazing Table

Feeds 15 to 20 people.
Requires about 6 to 8 feet of tabletop space.
Recommended grazing table package: The Onyx Opulence.

Events

For grand celebrations of 20+ people.
Recommended grazing table package: The Obsidian Event Table.
